# 🚨 Phishing Indicators Quick Reference

Use this guide to quickly evaluate any suspicious email.

---

## 🔴 HIGH RISK Indicators (Likely Phishing)

| # | Indicator | What to Look For |
|---|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Urgency Language** | "Immediately", "Suspended", "Act Now", "Warning!!!" |
| 2 | **Suspicious URL** | Misspelled domains, free site builders (webs.com), double slashes |
| 3 | **Credential Request** | Asking to "verify", "log in", "confirm" password or personal info |
| 4 | **Impersonation** | Claims to be Apple, HR, IT Helpdesk, a university, or government |
| 5 | **Crisis / Financial Lure** | COVID aid, financial hardship grants, unclaimed funds, job offers |

## 🟡 MEDIUM RISK Indicators (Suspicious)

| # | Indicator | What to Look For |
|---|-----------|-----------------|
| 6 | **Generic Greeting** | "Dear user@email.com" — uses your email instead of your name |
| 7 | **Filter Evasion** | Words with deliberate dots/spaces: "me ss ages", "dela.ye.d" |
| 8 | **Vague Sender** | "Admin Help Desk", "Assistant Professor" with no real identity |
| 9 | **Mismatched Branding** | Copyright notices or logos that don't match the claimed sender |
| 10 | **Unexpected Contact** | No prior relationship; unsolicited document or offer |

---

## ✅ Signs of a SAFE Email

- Uses your **real name** in the greeting
- Sent from a **known, verifiable email domain**
- **No links** — or only well-known organizational links
- **No requests** for credentials, payments, or personal data
- **Natural language** with correct grammar
- Clear **organizational context** (references prior conversations or roles)

---

## 🛑 The STOP-THINK-VERIFY Framework

```
STOP    → Do not click, reply, or download anything yet

THINK   → Is this expected? Am I being pressured? Does the sender make sense?

VERIFY  → Call the sender directly. Check the official website yourself.
```

---

## 📞 When to Report

Report immediately if the email:
- Asks for login credentials or payment
- Claims your account is suspended or compromised
- Offers unexpected financial rewards
- Contains a link you did not expect

**Report to:** Your IT Security / Help Desk team
